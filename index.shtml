Anna de la Paz Spanish Dance

My mission is to share the richness and diversity of Spanish dance with my classes and audiences in Rockland County, NY, and the surrounding areas. My dance performances, classes, school lectures & demonstrations and workshops in New York bring to life the many different styles of Spanish dance, including the courtly 18th century escuela bolera, the folkloric dances such as jota and muñeira, the elegant classical Spanish dances, and the powerful and deep interpretations of flamenco. Participants in my programs achieve a deep understanding and appreciation of the art form of Spanish dance throughout NY/NJ.
